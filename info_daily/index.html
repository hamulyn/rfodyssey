Daily Quest [ LEGACY ]

 

STEP BY STEP :
•  Go to GameCP > Login > Quest > Claim Ticket Quest
•  Go to Middle Sette NPC
•  Click Quest Then Click the Pick Button
If the Quest Is Successfully Taken / Picked It Will Enter In The Journal

TERMS AND CONDITIONS :
• Character Minimum Level 50
• Quest can be repeated every 12 hours after the quest is complete
• Quest Type Party

PROGRESS :
• Turncoat Gladius 20 at Sette Desert
• Turncoat Knight 15 at Sette Desert
• Turncoat Miler 15 at Sette Desert

REWARD :
• Legacy Lucky Box
• Contribution Point 2000
